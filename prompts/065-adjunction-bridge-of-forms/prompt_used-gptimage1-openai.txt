Two matching altars face each other across a narrow canal. Above the water, a pair of complementary bridges arc and meet at a polished joint: one broadening forms (left to right), the other tightening them (right to left). Objects carried across—wood blocks, glass bowls—transform smoothly in shape as they traverse and then return restored by the opposite span.
