Two facing cloisters across a watercourse; small boats ferry shapes from one side to the other, each trip matched by a quiet return carrying a different shape and a stamped seal. Over time, the traffic settles into a rhythm: to send this is to receive that. Lanterns mark units and counits at the piers. Marble, ivy, indigo water; reciprocity made architectural., landscape composition, 3:2 aspect ratio, 4x6 inch print
